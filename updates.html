New President. New Policies. Same Premier.

Change is a constant in Washington. Every four years, the ballots are tallied and the tides can turn. New people take office, new policies take effect and new administrations take over. But no matter what party or president we elect, one thing remains the same: Premier’s dedication to transforming healthcare together and driving improvement from the inside out.

From the latest MACRA ruling to alternative payment models to IT interoperability, our team of dedicated experts are on the ground in D.C. advocating for our members and making sure their voice is heard.

American healthcare is the best in the world on many measures, but we still have work to do. We won’t be satisfied until we cut costs, improve quality and give you, your neighbors and loved ones a better chance at a healthier life.

Our campaign to transform healthcare together never stops.
